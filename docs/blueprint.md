# **App Name**: Instala Solar Pro

## Core Features:

- Course Module Navigator: Interactive layout showcasing the 6 foundational modules with specialized visual states for desktop and mobile.
- Solar ROI Calculator Tool: A built-in reasoning tool that allows users to simulate the ROI of a R$19.90 investment against typical installation profits.
- Interactive Comparison Engine: Visual comparison table pitting the solar guide against traditional technical schools and YouTube tutorials.
- Testimonial WhatsApp Gallery: A faithfully recreated student success section with stylized chat captures and verifiable trust signals.
- Scarcity & Pulse Checkout: Dynamic 'CTA-pulse' system for the pricing section to emphasize time-sensitive special launch offers.
- FAQ Intelligent Accordion: Smooth, interactive dropdown system to address common concerns about solar installation requirements.
- Cross-Platform Response Optimizer: Fluid breakpoint management to ensure 100% fidelity on desktop, tablet, and mobile browsers.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Color: Energetic Orange (#FF6B00) to convey high energy and sun-related concepts.
- Background: Deep Charcoal (#0A0A0A) and Slate (#1A1A1A) to provide a premium, modern high-contrast aesthetic.
- Accent: Success Green (#25D366) used specifically for WhatsApp-themed sections and 'Free' bonus badges.
- Main font family is 'Inter', a grotesque sans-serif chosen for its machined, modern look and high readability at small sizes.
- Thin-stroke Lucide icons (2px stroke) colored in the primary orange for consistent technical and professional branding.
- A sequence of single-column blocks with constrained widths (max-w-6xl) ensuring a clean editorial flow across sections.
- Smooth 'fade-in-up' transitions for all sections as they enter the viewport to guide the user's attention through the funnel.
